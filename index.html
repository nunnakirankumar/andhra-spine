Meet Dr. Nagendra Palukuri

Expert Spine Specialist

Dr. Nagendra Palukuri is a renowned spine specialist with a commitment to precision care and modern techniques. He leads Andhra Spine Centre in Guntur with expertise in treating complex spinal disorders. With 20+ years of experience and a patient-centered approach, Dr. Palukuri has helped countless patients regain their mobility and quality of life through advanced spine care solutions.
